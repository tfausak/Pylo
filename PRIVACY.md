# Privacy Policy

**Pylo** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, 
analytics, or telemetry. The app operates entirely on your local network and 
communicates only with HomeKit controllers (such as the Home app) on the same 
network using the HomeKit Accessory Protocol.

No information is sent to the developer or any third party.

## Camera and Sensors

Pylo accesses the device camera, microphone, accelerometer, and ambient light 
sensor solely to provide HomeKit accessory functionality. Video, audio, and 
sensor data are streamed directly to your HomeKit hub over your local network 
using encrypted connections. This data is not recorded, stored, or transmitted 
elsewhere by the app, except when HomeKit Secure Video recording is enabled 
through the Home app, in which case recordings are managed entirely by Apple's 
HomeKit infrastructure.
